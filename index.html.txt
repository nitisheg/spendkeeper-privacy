<h3>SpendKeeper Privacy Policy</h3>

<p><strong>Effective Date:</strong> 08 July 2026</p>

<p>
SpendKeeper ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the SpendKeeper mobile application.
By using SpendKeeper, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
</p>

<h4>Information We Collect</h4>

<p>Depending on how you use the application, we may collect the following information:</p>

<ul>
<li>Name</li>
<li>Email Address</li>
<li>Google Account information (when using Google Sign-In)</li>
<li>Expense records entered by you</li>
<li>Income records entered by you</li>
<li>Budget information</li>
<li>Categories created by you</li>
<li>User preferences and settings</li>
<li>Biometric authentication preference (stored only on your device)</li>
</ul>

<h4>How We Use Your Information</h4>

<p>Your information is used to:</p>

<ul>
<li>Create and manage your account.</li>
<li>Authenticate your identity securely.</li>
<li>Store and synchronize your financial records.</li>
<li>Generate expense summaries and reports.</li>
<li>Improve application performance and user experience.</li>
<li>Respond to customer support requests.</li>
<li>Maintain application security.</li>
</ul>

<h4>Firebase Services</h4>

<p>
SpendKeeper uses Google Firebase services, including:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Firebase Authentication</li>
<li>Cloud Firestore</li>
</ul>

<p>
Firebase securely stores your account information and application data.
For more information, please visit:
</p>

<p>
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
</p>

<h4>Google Sign-In</h4>

<p>
If you choose to sign in using your Google account, we may receive:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Your Name</li>
<li>Email Address</li>
<li>Profile Picture (if available)</li>
</ul>

<p>
This information is used solely for authentication and identification within SpendKeeper.
</p>

<h4>Biometric Authentication</h4>

<p>
SpendKeeper optionally supports biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or Fingerprint) to securely unlock the application.
Your biometric information never leaves your device and is never stored on our servers.
Authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system.
</p>

<h4>Data Storage</h4>

<p>
Your financial data is securely stored using Google Firebase Cloud Firestore.
We retain your information only while your account remains active or as required to provide our services.
</p>

<h4>Permissions Used</h4>

<ul>
<li><strong>Internet</strong> – Required for authentication and cloud synchronization.</li>
<li><strong>Notifications</strong> – Used for reminders and important notifications (if enabled).</li>
<li><strong>Biometric Authentication</strong> – Used to securely unlock the application (if enabled).</li>
</ul>

<h4>How We Protect Your Information</h4>

<p>
We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
Only authenticated users can access their own stored information.
</p>
