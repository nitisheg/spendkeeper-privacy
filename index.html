SpendKeeper Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08 July 2026

SpendKeeper ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the SpendKeeper mobile application. By using SpendKeeper, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the application, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Google Account information (when using Google Sign-In)
  • Expense records entered by you
  • Income records entered by you
  • Budget information
  • Categories created by you
  • User preferences and settings
  • Biometric authentication preference (stored only on your device)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate your identity securely.
  • Store and synchronize your financial records.
  • Generate expense summaries and reports.
  • Improve application performance and user experience.
  • Respond to customer support requests.
  • Maintain application security.

Firebase Services

SpendKeeper uses Google Firebase services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore

Firebase securely stores your account information and application data. For more information, please visit:

https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in using your Google account, we may receive:

  • Your Name
  • Email Address
  • Profile Picture (if available)

This information is used solely for authentication and identification within SpendKeeper.

Biometric Authentication

SpendKeeper optionally supports biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or Fingerprint) to securely unlock the application. Your biometric information never leaves your device and is never stored on our servers. Authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system.

Data Storage

Your financial data is securely stored using Google Firebase Cloud Firestore. We retain your information only while your account remains active or as required to provide our services.

Permissions Used

  • Internet – Required for authentication and cloud synchronization.
  • Notifications – Used for reminders and important notifications (if enabled).
  • Biometric Authentication – Used to securely unlock the application (if enabled).

How We Protect Your Information

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Only authenticated users can access their own stored information.

Third-Party Services

SpendKeeper uses trusted third-party services, including:

  • Google Firebase
  • Google Sign-In

These providers maintain their own privacy policies governing how they process your information.

Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account.
  • Request deletion of your stored personal information.

Delete Your Account

You may request deletion of your SpendKeeper account and associated data by contacting us at:

apps@eglogics.com

After verifying your request, we will permanently delete your account and associated data within a reasonable timeframe, unless we are required by law to retain certain information.

Children's Privacy

SpendKeeper is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date. We encourage users to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
